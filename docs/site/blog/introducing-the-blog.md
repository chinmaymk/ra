# Introducing the ra blog

<span class="blog-date">March 30, 2026</span>

We're adding a blog to the ra docs. This is where we'll post release announcements, feature deep dives, tips and tricks, and anything else worth sharing about the project.

## What to expect

- **Release notes** — What changed, why it changed, and how to upgrade.
- **Feature deep dives** — Longer walkthroughs of new capabilities.
- **Tips and tricks** — Patterns, recipes, and workflows we find useful.

Stay tuned for more.
